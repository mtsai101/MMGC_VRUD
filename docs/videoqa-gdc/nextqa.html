Introduction

The videos in NExT-QA are featuring object interaction in daily life and are sourced from Flickr. Although we did not restrict the video content, the videos are mostly about family time, kid playing, social gatherings, activities, pets and performances. We manually annotated an average 10 question-answer pairs for each video covering causal, temporal and descriptive questions. The distractor answers in multi-choice QA are retrieved from the dataset, and then go through a manual check to fix those problematic distractor answers, so as to make sure that they are challenging enough to disambiguate without understanding the videos.

Data Statistics

NExT-QA contains totally 5431 videos with average length of 44s and about 52K manually annotated questions grouped into causal (50.3%),temporal (28.3%) and descriptive (21.4%) questions. We randomly split the data into train/val/test set according to videos: 3861/570/1000, in which the 1000 test videos are held out for peer-comparison.
